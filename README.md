## 🚀 About Me

I’m an **AI Engineer at Zieers Systems**, focused on building **LLM-powered applications, Generative AI systems, RAG pipelines, AI Agents, and intelligent automation solutions**.

Currently, I’m working on **LexQπ**, an AI-powered Indian legal intelligence platform designed to improve **legal research, legal question answering, judgment retrieval, and Legal NLP workflows** using hybrid search, vector databases, fine-tuned language models, and scalable backend systems.

My work involves designing **AI workflows**, integrating **Large Language Models**, building **retrieval pipelines**, preparing large-scale legal datasets, developing backend APIs, and improving the accuracy of legal question-answering systems.

I have worked with a **dual-model AI architecture** combining:

* **InLegalBERT** for legal text understanding and semantic representation
* **QLoRA fine-tuned Mistral 7B** for generative legal responses

I also have experience processing **20M+ Indian court judgments** through OCR extraction, text cleaning, chunking, metadata preparation, and corpus building for production-ready AI systems.
