<h2 align="center">⚖️ Current Project: LexQπ</h2>

<h3 align="center">AI-Powered Indian Legal Intelligence Platform</h3>

**LexQπ** is an AI-powered legal intelligence platform focused on improving Indian legal research, judgment retrieval, and legal question answering.

My work includes:

* Building **RAG pipelines** for legal question answering
* Designing **hybrid search** using keyword search + vector search
* Using **InLegalBERT** for legal text understanding
* Working with **QLoRA fine-tuned Mistral 7B** for generative legal responses
* Processing **20M+ Indian court judgments**
* Performing OCR extraction, text cleaning, chunking, and metadata preparation
* Building large-scale legal corpora for production-ready AI systems
* Developing backend APIs for AI-powered legal workflows
* Improving retrieval quality, answer accuracy, and response reliability
